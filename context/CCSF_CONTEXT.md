## What CCSF is

CCSF is the student leadership board of Central church's English Ministry (EM),
serving two campuses: **CMU and Pitt**. It sits alongside the DGL team (small
group leaders) and under church leadership (Pastor Graham, elders). The Korean
Ministry (KM) is a sibling congregation — several events are **joint EM/KM**.
Freshmen intake each fall is ~60; the church-wide Messenger "Central Chat" hit
247/250 members in Oct 2025 (the platform-limit pain that motivated this app).

## The annual calendar (recurring rhythm)

### Summer (May–Aug)
- **First Summer DG** late May → **last Summer DG** late June (DGs run through summer)
- **Mission trip** (2025: Japan, Jul 5–16)
- **EM/KM Joint Praise Night** — early/mid Aug, kicks off the year
- Board planning starts in **June** — venue reservations for all of Welcome Week happen in June; Coffeehouse forms go out in **July**

### Welcome Week (mid/late Aug — the flagship multi-event sequence)
Anchored to CMU/Pitt orientation (starts ~Aug 17–18) and first day of classes (~Aug 24–25):
- **Popsicle / Ice Cream Socials** — one per campus (CMU @ the Cut, Pitt @ Towers/Cathy Lawn), 12–2 PM, ~$40–50, 20+ volunteers, ~30 newcomers. No table allowed at CMU; needs cooler/ice packs + driver.
- **Pitt Fall Involvement Fair** (The Pete), 1–4 PM — tabling with banner, poster, info cards
- **Game Night / Game Day** (CMU, Danforth Conference Room — book early in summer), 7–10 PM, ~100 turnout; Switch + board games for ~6 tables, snacks ~$20
- **Sports at Cathy / Sports on campus** (Pitt Cathy Lawn / CMU Cut), 2–4 PM; balls, volleyball nets, spikeball, picnic blankets
- **First Praise Night** (Central Church) 6/7–9 PM — reserve church in June; contact praise leader 2 weeks ahead ("they usually forget"); DGL president preps dinner + prayer topics
- **Welcoming Night** (Central Church, Saturday, 5:30–9 PM) — THE anchor event, see below
- **Church Tag-Alongs** — upperclassmen bring freshmen to first Sunday service (8:15 AM meet)
- **Outreach meeting** during the week (plan volunteer orgs + campus EV)
- **The FAIR** (CMU, ~Sep 3, 4:30–6:30 PM @ the Cut) — register via CMU REACH in August, 6+ volunteers
- 2026–27 tweak: socials renamed "Popsicle Social" on both campuses; Game Day moved to 3–6 PM; sports on both campuses; Welcoming Night run by **sophomores**

### Fall
- **First DGs** — first Friday after Welcome Week (Sep 5, 2025; Sep 11, 2026). DGs then run **every Friday** 6–9 PM through the semester (last DGs mid-April).
- **Churchwide Picnic** — ~Sep 20–21, joint EM/KM, volunteers from DGs
- **Coffeehouse** — Sep 14 (2025) / Oct 4 (2026). Talent show, see per-event detail.
- **Women's Retreat** — late Oct (Oct 23, 2026)
- **Turkeybowl** — Nov flag football. 2025: girls + guys on Nov 8/15. 2026–27: **Girls Oct 31, Guys Nov 7** (separate dates). See per-event detail.
- Winter break ~Dec 14

- **Early morning prayer** resumes (Jan)
- **Men's Retreat** — Feb (2026: Feb 13–15; 2027: ~Jan 29/Feb 5). CCSF now co-plans (retreat leads want one YA + one undergrad).
- **GAN/SAN — Guys/Sisters Appreciation Night** — Feb (Feb 21, 2026; Feb 20, 2027 "Guys Appreciation Night")

- **Friday DGs** (6–9 PM) + **Prayer Meetings** — whole board helps, L.O.C.K closes

- **CCSF board meeting** — biweekly, Sat ~10 AM at church

## Per-event operational detail

### Welcoming Night (the biggest single event)
- **Plan starting June**; reserve church space in June; volunteer form out ~Jun 21; groups finalized ~Aug 1; signup forms to freshmen the Monday before; "expect things to be done 2 days late (esp. with forms)"
- Budget ~$200–300 actual 2025 spend: gifts $28.59 (yellow boxes), decor $46 (paint/tape/markers), food $127 (noodles, salad, soda)
- Turnout 2025: **45 of 61 freshmen**; prepared for 60
- **Volunteer group structure** (each with a leader): Praise (6), Decor/Gifts (~12), Food (~9), Games (game-team facilitators), Welcoming (door), 2 MCs
- Run of show: 1:30 praise practice → 4:00 kitchen opens → 5:30 freshmen arrive → 6:00 dinner → 7:00 praise → 7:30 intros (CCSF intro 7–7:30) → 8:00 games (CCSF+DGLs clean kitchen during) → 8:45 gifts/photos → 9:15 cleanup → 9:45 doors closed
- QR-code signup printed; photographers/videographers recruited; freshmen added to Central Chat at every welcoming event

### Coffeehouse
- Talent show: performances + praise + testimony. 2025: Sunday **5–7 PM at Rangos Hall (CMU)**; doors/setup 1:30, performance practices 2–4:30
- **Form pipeline**: interest form out ~Jul 23 → close ~Aug 1 → choose groups at Aug 9 board meeting → performance-leaders form ~Aug 25 → volunteer leaders delegated ~Aug 27 → request sound equipment (church + CMU **Ideate** loan program) ~Aug 29 → IG post ~Sep 1 → confirm sound ~Sep 3 → leader check-ins the week before
- 2025 line-up (7 acts, with per-act practice slots + durations): String Ensemble 12m, Dance 3m, skit "Blood" 12m, Praise 6m, Testimony 15m, skit "First Love" 10m, Sunday School Praise 10m; MC intros, intermission, closing; photos + cleanup at 7
- Interest form fields: name, school (CMU/PITT/YA), year, phone, serve-as (General Performer / Leaders / Outreach-Welcoming), performance type, existing group + members. ~29 responses in 2025.
- Each act tracked with: leaders, members list, duration, "motive + Bible verse", stage equipment needs
- **Equipment manifest** (church + Ideate): mixer, snake, speakers ×2, floor monitor, mics ×5 (+5 SM-58s from Ideate), mic stands ×4, DI boxes ×3+3, ¼" + XLR cables, pianos ×2 / guitar / cajon from people
- Volunteer teams: Performance, Welcoming, Baking (baking form!), Sound. Ops footprint: banner, post-its, water. Leave venue by 7.

### Turkeybowl
- Nov flag football; **girls and guys divisions on separate dates** (2026–27). Venue: outdoor field + tries for Pitt Sports Dome (girls inside when shared)
- 2025 scale: **189 shirts for $1,528**, sold at **$10 each** (excess funds → water/Gatorade); 65 non-QB guys (~18/team, made 6 teams, YAs mingled in), 27 non-QB undergrad girls (YA girls integrated, not separate team)
- Signups released right after Coffeehouse; shirt form (sizes/names); need 3 guys + 1–4 girls footballs; awards; maybe donated goods from Pitt
- Book venue as early as possible (2025 target date was already taken)

### Retreats
- **Women's Retreat** (Oct), **Men's Retreat** (Feb), **EM Retreat** (Mar); Men's Camping also exists as a separate hangout
- Retreat leaders (one YA + one undergrad) run program; CCSF co-plans/handles logistics

### Appreciation nights (SAN/GAN)
- Feb; guys plan SAN (Sisters Appreciation Night), girls plan GAN; flowers + food + venue. 2026: SAN responsibilities picked up by Inreach.

### Membership / church-side duties
- **Membership/baptism Sundays**: CCSF buys + arranges **flowers** (request through church — can't expense via CMU/Pitt)
- Weekly **announcements** at church + chat + slides for PM/DG/Sunday service
- **Merch** (fundraising) + bake sales; big events funded by the schools (CMU/Pitt org funding), rest by church; P-card signups mid-Aug (CMU)
- Ideas floated: intergrade hangouts, DG hangouts, lock-ins, biking/rock climbing, IM sports, Central Assassins, Superbowl watch party (in-church)

## Communication & tooling pain points (why Central exists)
- Messenger "Central Chat" capped at 250 (247 used Oct 2025) — considered Slack/Discord/community chats
- Contact collection via Google Form; universal mailing list wanted; weekly reminder cadence manual
- Everything ran on scattered Google Docs/Sheets/Forms: per-event workbooks, signup forms, budget lines in meeting notes, rotation grids in a spreadsheet
- Handoff between boards is a single transition doc — "this only covers SOME not ALL of the responsibilities"
